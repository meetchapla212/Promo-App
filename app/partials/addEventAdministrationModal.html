An event administrator will be able to manage your event, keep an eye on tickets and payments, and keep things running smoothly.
They will have access to your event until the event is marked as "closed" on The Promo App .

help

Any event administrator you add will be able to :

  • View the event in their "manage events" list
  • Edit/view/add new ticket types
  • Edit/delete the event
  • Add new administrators to the event
  • Scan attendees' tickets
  • View and change all ticket types (Both Paid and Free options)
