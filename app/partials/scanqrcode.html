How to Scan Your QR Code

Open your camera app and hold your device over the QR code. Hold your phone steady for 2 - 3 seconds, making sure to capture the full code on your screen.

When you scan a QR code with us for the first time, you'll be asked to give our QR scanner permission to access the camera app on your mobile device. After that, you'll be able to scan any new QR codes without any additional security prompts.
