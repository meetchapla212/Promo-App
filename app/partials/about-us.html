about us

The Promo App is an event-based social network that tracks over 30 million events worldwide!

By signing up, users can browse a live feed of public events with real-time event information, or create and share their own events.

Users can access the web app using any device, from anywhere

Connecting with friends, family, and people with similar interests.

Some of our most exciting features include

Sell Tickets

The ability to sell tickets to your event straight from the platform - there's no need to pay for a more expensive ticketing service.

Manage Events

An easy-to-use dashboard that helps you add and manage all your event details.

Track Sales

A simple way to track your sales and keep an eye on how well your event is doing.
We are constantly adding new features, making event management
better than ever before. Keep checking back to see how we grow!

George O'Neil

Founder of Promo App George is the creative brain behind the Promo App. With his focus on small communities, he is passionate about helping neighborhoods thrive.
